TOURISM

Tourism in Panama is a big part of the economy. Panama being in the center of america makes it a great spot for people from around the world to go for vacations. The main areas of tourism in the country is to island, beaches and the outskirts of the country. These are some of the best places to visit while in Panama:


Panama Viejo

This monument is the oldest building in the whole city because it was the original city before it was destroyed by pirate Henry Morgan. This is a very big tourist attraction because of how well the structure has been maintained since the 1500's. This place is great for those who love to learn about history.


Panama Canal

For almost every tourist this is a must stop. Afterall the Panama Canal is one of the Seven wonders of the modern world. It's considered to be one of the greatest engineering pieces of the 20th century.


Ave. Balboa

For most people an Avenue is not really a tourstist place. However, in Ave. Balboa Panama gets the best sunlight for picture and often the sky becomes pinkish and allows you to see the magnificent skyline. This is a great spot for people to take pictures.


Casco Viejo

Casco Viejo at night it's the most lively place in the city. This old-style looking street is home to Panama's night life. The best restaurants and bars are in the area. It's a great place to see how houses and buildings were built in the older days, tipically a location people with architecture interests go to.


Chiriqui

8 hours away from the city lies Chiriqui. Chiriqui is a completly different feel from the city. It gives you the feel of nature that the city doesn't give you. This is a good toursit palce because there is so much to do. From hiking "Volcan Baru" all the way to zip-line. Chiriqui is also home to the most expensive coffee in the world "Panama Geisha". This is a must go place for those coffee lovers.
